How to Submit

Follow these steps to submit your manuscript for consideration in UJSMS.

1

Prepare Your Manuscript

Ensure your manuscript meets all formatting and structural requirements outlined in this guide. Remove all author identifying information from the manuscript file for double-blind review. Prepare your cover letter separately.

2

Check Submission Requirements

Review the checklist below and confirm your manuscript is within the word count, properly formatted, includes all required sections, and your reference list is complete and consistent.

3

Submit via Email

Send your manuscript as a Microsoft Word (.docx) attachment to submissions@ujsms.com. Include your cover letter, author details form, and any supplementary files.

4

Desk Review (1–2 Weeks)

The editorial team will conduct an initial desk review to confirm the submission meets scope and quality thresholds. You will receive acknowledgement within 5 business days and a desk decision within 2 weeks.

5

Peer Review (4–8 Weeks)

Manuscripts that pass desk review are sent to at least two independent peer reviewers. The double-blind process ensures neither authors nor reviewers are aware of each other's identities.

6

Revision & Final Decision

Authors will receive reviewer comments and have 4 weeks to revise and resubmit. A final editorial decision will be communicated within 2 weeks of resubmission. Accepted articles enter production immediately.

Eligibility

Submission Requirements

✅ Submission Checklist

✓Manuscript is original and not under consideration elsewhere
✓All author details removed from the manuscript file
✓Abstract of 150–250 words included
✓5–8 keywords provided
✓Word count within specified range for manuscript type
✓References follow APA 7th Edition format
✓Tables and figures properly labelled
✓Ethical approval obtained where applicable
✓Conflict of interest statement included
✓Cover letter prepared separately

Manuscript Format

Formatting Instructions

📄 Document Format

SettingSpecification
File formatMicrosoft Word (.docx)
Page sizeA4
Margins2.5 cm all sides
FontTimes New Roman, 12pt
Line spacingDouble (2.0)
Paragraph indent1.27 cm first line
HeadingsBold, Title Case (Section), Italic (Subsection)

📊 Tables & Figures

  • ✓ Tables numbered sequentially: Table 1, Table 2…
  • ✓ Table title above; figure caption below
  • ✓ Figures as high-resolution images (min. 300 dpi)
  • ✓ Provide editable data for all statistical charts
  • ✓ Source acknowledged for all non-original data
  • ✓ Statistical significance values reported (p < 0.05)

📝 Manuscript Structure

1.Title Page (submitted separately): title, all author names, affiliations, corresponding author email
2.Abstract (150–250 words): background, methods, results, conclusions
3.Keywords: 5–8 terms, alphabetical order
4.Introduction: background, research gap, objectives, significance
5.Literature Review / Theoretical Framework
6.Methodology: design, data, instruments, analysis approach
7.Results and Discussion
8.Conclusion, Limitations, and Future Research
9.Acknowledgements (optional)
10.References (APA 7th Edition)

📚 Reference Style (APA 7th)

All references must follow APA 7th Edition. Examples:

Journal: Author, A. A. (Year). Title. Journal Name, Vol(Issue), pp–pp.
Book: Author, A. A. (Year). Book title. Publisher.
Chapter: Author, A. A. (Year). Chapter title. In E. Editor (Ed.), Book title (pp. xx–xx). Publisher.
Quality Assurance

Peer Review Process

UJSMS employs a rigorous double-blind peer review to ensure scholarly excellence in every published article.

📨
Submission

Manuscript received and logged. Acknowledgement sent within 5 business days.

🔍
Desk Review

Editor-in-Chief screens for scope fit, quality, and adherence to guidelines. Decision in 1–2 weeks.

👥
Peer Review

Two independent expert reviewers assess the manuscript. Double-blind. Completed in 4–8 weeks.

✅
Decision & Publication

Accept / Minor Revision / Major Revision / Reject. Accepted articles published within 4 weeks.
