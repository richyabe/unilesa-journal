Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the review process take?

From submission to final decision typically takes 8–12 weeks, depending on reviewer availability and revision turnaround.

Can I submit a manuscript that has been previously rejected?

Yes. If your manuscript has been significantly revised in response to previous reviewer comments, you may resubmit. Please indicate this in your cover letter.

Are there any submission or publication fees?

No. UJSMS charges no submission, review, or article processing fees. Publication is completely free for all authors.

Can I track the status of my manuscript?

Yes. You will receive email notifications at each stage of the review process. You can also contact the editorial office for a status update after 6 weeks.
